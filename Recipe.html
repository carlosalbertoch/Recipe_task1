Chocolate cake

Here is a fast and easy version of the chocolate cake recipe. Enjoy!

Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingredients

Black chocolate: 200g

Sugar:150g

Butter:200g

Eggs:4

Fluor:80g

Instructions

  1. Melt the chocolate and butter together
  2. Mix the sugar, eggs and the flour
  3. Mix everyting to get a smooth dough
  4. Bake in the oven
